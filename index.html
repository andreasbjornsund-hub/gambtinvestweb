About

Andreas Parr Bjørnsund is a Norwegian business angel investing through Gambit Invest AS, focused on identifying and supporting exceptional early-stage technology companies.

In chess, a gambit is a calculated sacrifice, giving up material to gain a stronger position. That same contrarian philosophy drives my investment approach. I bring more than capital to my portfolio companies: strategic guidance, industry connections, and operational expertise to help founders navigate the critical early stages of company building.

Investment Thesis

Every strong position requires conviction. I seek contrarian opportunities at the intersection of innovation, scalability, and Nordic values.

Early Stage Niche Tech

I focus on early-stage companies operating in specialized technology niches where deep expertise creates sustainable competitive advantages.

High Gross Margin Products

I prioritize companies with products that demonstrate strong unit economics and the potential for significant margin expansion at scale.

International Footprint

My portfolio companies are built for global markets from day one, with products and services that transcend geographic boundaries.

Incentivised Team

I back founders and teams with significant skin in the game, aligned incentives, and the drive to build enduring companies.
